About

Leading with musical rigor and human warmth.

Carrie Tennant is a Canadian conductor and educator recognized for youth choir excellence, singer development, community building, artistic innovation, and leadership training.

Based in Vancouver, her practice spans ensemble direction, guest conducting, workshops, festivals, mentorship, conference speaking, and collaborations that invite singers into deeper artistry and agency.

Youth Voices

Developing confident singers and creative citizens.

Founded by Carrie Tennant in 2013, Vancouver Youth Choir has grown into a respected choral home for young singers, emphasizing artistry, excellence, care, and contribution.
